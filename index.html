Description

I’m a software engineering student at Concordia University in Montreal with a passion for game development. As president of the Concordia Game Development Club (CGD), I’m dedicated to fostering collaboration and innovation in the game dev community.

I’m also an independent game developer with hands-on experience from over half a dozen game jams, where I’ve honed my skills in rapid prototyping and creative problem-solving. My interests include system design, procedurally generated content, and gameplay balancing. Plus, I’ve contributed to the game dev community by creating tools for the Godot engine. I’m all about pushing the limits of what games can be!
